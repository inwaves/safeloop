# Research Direction: Harness Search for Safer Agents

This document mirrors the initial project framing from the knowledge base.

## Core question

Can we automatically discover agent harnesses that make a fixed base model behave more safely, without simply blocking actions or adding external oversight, and do those safety improvements generalize out of distribution?

## Dated notes

### 2026-05-04

Initial framing: search/evolve the safest harness on in-distribution agent safety tasks, then test whether safety improvements generalize out of distribution. This is the cleaner version of the Agar thought: not just "evaluate agents," but treat the harness itself as an optimizable inference-time safety intervention.
